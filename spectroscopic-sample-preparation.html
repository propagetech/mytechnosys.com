Spectroscopic Sample Preparation Equipment’s

HOME / PRODUCTS /  SPECTROSCOPIC SAMPLE PREPARATION EQUIPMENT’S
Sample preparation is an very important factor to get right spark and get accurate results in an Optical Emission Spectrometer using Spark / Arc / Glow Discharge excitation. It is important to have faster surface preparation for spectrometer analysis by means of Grinding or Milling operation for Ferrous and Non Ferrous material.

Metkon has a wide range of practical solution product starting from Disc Grinders compact table top model to heavy duty Dual Disc Grinder, Closed Chamber Pendulum Grinder, Closed Chamber Milling Machine with Automatic and Manual Control to the Fully Automatic Preparation equipment for In-Line Spectrocopic Preparation System for large steel plants. Every machine is designed to have highest safety for Operator, fast preparation with minimal contamination, produce repeatable results.
