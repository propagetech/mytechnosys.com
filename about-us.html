About Us

Attaining the Status of the most prefered solution provider in the field of material testing.

HOME /  ABOUT US
Technosys is one of the leading suppliers of material testing instruments and metrology products. We represent the market leaders in the field of metallography, mechanical testing, material testing, dimensional measurement, corrosion testing instruments. Technosys team is engaged in providing value added services to the instruments supplied also provides Turnkey solutions.

Key Products: Microscopes, Metallography Products, Mechanical Testing Instruments, Hardness Testing Instruments, Corrosion Test Chambers, Measuring Instruments like Profile Projectors, Video Measuring Instruments, Non Contact Measurement etc….

Our Mission

To become the most preferred solution provider in the field of materials testing, our supplies and services should be the best solution for the customer. Our approach to business should be driven by the customers need and to provide value-added services to help customers to become a pioneer in their field. Expand the horizon to become Global Leader.

Technosys has the distributorship of the flagship companies to supply material testing equipment and measuring instruments. We provide sales and after sales service support for the entire range of our products. We have a group of factory trained engineers to provide installation, training and application support on the supplied instruments.

Quick, Responsive and Trustworthy
We listen to our customers, focus on their needs and react fast to their requests. Technosys is accessible, flexible, trustworthy and easy to work with.
